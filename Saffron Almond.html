About Saffron Almond Oil

A product of finest quality from Spain(Oil) and Kashmir(Saffron)

The most important health benefits of sweet almond oil include protecting the skin from damage, moisturizing the face, lightening the skin, reducing inflammation, preventing premature aging, boosting hair health, and strengthening the nails, among others. The blending of Kesar or Saffron with this oil enhances its benefits greatly.

This oil contains vitamins A, B1, B2 and B6 which helps to nourish and protect the skin. Used for chapped, irritated skin, eczema, cradie cap and to soften wrinkles. Saffron is an anti-oxidant and anti-depressant. It is used to treat diabetes and when taken with milk it relieves cold.
