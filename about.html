About Us

RS Fragrances was founded in 1998. RS Fragrances is one of India’s leading distillers of Natural Essential Oils. The company is situated in Ootacamund (Ooty), the only place in India where Natural Nilgiri Oil (Eucalyptus Oil) is distilled. The Nilgiris are surrounded by the magnificent Blue mountains and have one of the largest collections of medicinal plants and trees. Our Business is bounded by ethical values which have aided our growth and we provide only the highest quality products to our customers.

R S Fragrances has been forefront in the field of Research and Development. We have our own in house testing and R&D facilities. R S Fragrances is a leading supplier to many multinational companies, perfumery houses, pharmaceutical industries,also retail outlets in ooty.
